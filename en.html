Your skipper

Jérôme

Professional Skipper · Boat Tours Hendaye

Born into the Guilharretxe tradition, a true son of the Basque Country, a sailor from father to son for generations, Jérôme grew up with his feet in the water and his eyes on the horizon. For him, sailing is not just a profession — it is an inherited calling and a passion that has taken him across many oceans, both personally and professionally.

A professional skipper certified by the prestigious Maritime School of La Rochelle — which has trained France's greatest sailors — and also trained in scuba diving and marine life observation, Jérôme shares with his passengers a deep knowledge of the marine ecosystem and the secrets of the Basque Coast. On board, his enthusiasm is contagious: he will be delighted to show you the most beautiful corners of this exceptional coastline.
